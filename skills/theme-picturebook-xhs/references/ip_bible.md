# Fixed IP Bible

Use this fixed IP for children's picturebooks unless the user explicitly asks to redesign it.

## World

`暖芽小院` is a gentle picturebook world beside a small garden, a kitchen window, a reading rug, and a winding path to forests, fields, ponds, markets, and festival streets. Every theme can enter the story as a new visitor, object, season, plant, animal, tradition, or small mystery discovered by the recurring characters.

Core mood: warm, healing, curious, safe, childlike, nature-friendly, family-readable.

## Recurring Characters

### 小暖

- role: main child explorer and emotional anchor
- type: human child
- age feeling: 6 years old
- silhouette: small round child silhouette, slightly oversized head, soft cheeks
- face: round face, black bob haircut, small curved eyebrows, bright dark eyes
- colors: warm cream, soft chestnut, tiny red accent
- clothing: cream cotton pinafore, chestnut long-sleeve shirt, red star hairpin, small brown crossbody notebook bag
- personality: curious, gentle, brave after thinking, asks clear childlike questions
- signature prop: notebook bag with a tiny leaf-shaped clasp
- expression range: wonder, careful thinking, mild worry, proud smile, quiet joy
- forbidden drift: do not change hairstyle, hairpin, notebook bag, or main clothing colors

### 团团

- role: loyal companion and comic warmth
- type: small plush-like bear friend
- age feeling: childlike friend
- silhouette: round bear body, short limbs, soft belly, small rounded ears
- face: button-like dark eyes, tiny oval nose, simple warm smile
- colors: honey brown body, cream muzzle and belly
- clothing: moss-green knitted scarf
- personality: kind, food-loving, a little cautious, good at noticing sounds and smells
- signature prop: moss-green scarf with one stitched yellow dot
- expression range: cozy smile, surprised round eyes, shy worry, relieved laugh
- forbidden drift: do not make the bear realistic, fierce, large, or change scarf color

### 芽芽

- role: nature guide and imagination spark
- type: cute flying little fairy
- age feeling: timeless but childlike
- silhouette: thumb-sized, pear-shaped body, two leaf sprouts on head, tiny translucent leaf wings, always light enough to hover or fly
- face: simple dot eyes, tiny mouth, soft blush
- colors: pale green body, fresh leaf-green sprouts, tiny translucent leaf wings, small warm yellow glow
- clothing: no clothing; natural leaf texture only
- personality: patient, observant, poetic but simple, knows small facts about plants, weather, and seasons
- signature prop: tiny translucent leaf wings and faint warm glow around leaf tips
- expression range: calm smile, focused observation, excited sparkle, sleepy droop
- forbidden drift: do not remove wings or flying ability, do not add human clothing, do not change sprout head shape, do not make the fairy human-sized or realistic

### 小舟

- role: practical maker and problem solver, optional fourth character
- type: human child
- age feeling: 7 years old
- silhouette: slim child silhouette, tidy posture
- face: short dark hair, clear eyes, small focused smile
- colors: denim blue, white, sunflower yellow accent
- clothing: denim-blue jacket, white shirt, yellow pencil tucked behind one ear
- personality: organized, inventive, patient, likes making tools and maps
- signature prop: small folding ruler
- expression range: focused, amused, surprised, satisfied
- forbidden drift: use only when needed; do not replace 小暖 as the emotional lead

## Character Use Rules

- Use 小暖 plus 1-2 companions by default.
- Use 团团 for cozy, festival, food, family, animal, and friendship themes.
- Use 芽芽 for plant, season, nature, weather, science, and observation themes.
- Use 小舟 only when the story needs building, measuring, mapping, repairing, or planning.
- Temporary animals, plants, festival figures, neighbors, or family members may appear as side characters, but they must not replace the core cast.

## Visual Continuity Rules

- Keep the same silhouettes, colors, face features, clothing, and signature props across every image.
- Add theme props lightly, such as lanterns, seed packets, flower crowns, rain boots, or small baskets.
- Remove or minimize theme props if they obscure the fixed character anchors.
- Keep scale child-friendly: 小暖 and 小舟 are children, 团团 is small enough to hug, 芽芽 is tiny enough to sit on a leaf or notebook and can hover beside flowers, windows, or page objects.

## Setting Rules

Default starting point is 暖芽小院. Move outward only when the theme benefits from it:

- festivals: kitchen window, courtyard table, street lanterns, family doorway
- animals: garden path, pond edge, meadow, forest clearing
- plants: seed shelf, flower bed, greenhouse corner, field path
- seasons/weather: window, roof eaves, puddle path, snowy yard
- emotions/habits: reading rug, bedroom corner, breakfast table, quiet bench

Every new setting should still feel connected to 暖芽小院.

## Forbidden Drift

- Do not rename the IP world without user approval.
- Do not replace the recurring cast with one-off protagonists.
- Do not change core character species, clothing, colors, or signature props.
- Do not make the style dark, frightening, realistic horror, glossy 3D, anime battle, or adult editorial.
- Do not turn the story into a knowledge card deck; keep it a narrative picturebook.
